Breaking the Sound Barrier

Breaking the sound barrier was a major achievement in aviation history. It allowed planes to travel faster than the speed of sound, opening the door to new possibilities in flight. This breakthrough helped scientists and engineers create faster and more advanced aircraft, changing how we explore the skies and pushing the limits of what humans can achieve in the air.
